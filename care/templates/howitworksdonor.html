How it Works?

About this website:


As COVID-19 pandemic is at it's full swing, majority of the people are facing crisis for meeting the daily/basic needs. So as to help in the way we could, our team has developed this website for like-minded people around us. Here this site will be a medium between the person interested to seek the help and the one interested to help. Only wearing a mask or sanitizing can't provide a living for many. Though they are essential, students have to complete there education to get a job, daily wagers have to get a work to feed their family. And for all of these many would required a financial aid. And this is the reason, that this website came into existance.


Process:

For Donor:

1. First click on Donor Register/Login Button in Home Page.
2. If new to this website, click on Sign up link.
3. Login with username and password.
4. Go To Profile.
5. Fill all the details.
6. Click on Submit Button. A success message will be displayed.
7. Go To Receiver Details to see the information for the people who need help and contact them via email or phone number in accordance with the type of donation they preferred for.
8. Go To Dashboard to Explore and Donate in Government and Private Schemes to help the poor.
9. Click on the Log out button to get back to main site.
